When technology helps

Mattersmith helps improve the adeptness of day-to-day practice, whether you are external counsel or running an in-house legal business.


Knowledge

Knowledge is an expert system framework to record and facilitate the retrieval of trusted knowledge and data. Knowledge enables the visualisation of contracts, the relationships between them, templates and policies, and the automation of a set of rules or suggestions that are suitable for a particular task.

Review

Review systematically searches unstructured legal text. Frequency counts show the number of times a concept appears in the documents. A report compares the content of a number of documents reviewed simultaneously. Users can seamlessly access Knowledge to find out how to respond to an issue in a document and to see suggested language with which it may be amended.

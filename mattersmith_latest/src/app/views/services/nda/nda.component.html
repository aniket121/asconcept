Non-disclosure agreements

Mattersmith NDA is a fully enabled application of our AI-supported platform to facilitate the simultaneous review of multiple non-disclosure agreements and confidentiality undertakings.


When volume is a problem

A volume of NDAs can rapidly become a problem. Mattersmith provides viable options: outsource the problem or use technology to improve productivity, make life easier and accelerate the provision of legal advice. We’ve hands-on experience of NDAs every day from capital markets to commercial. The solution may be no more than applying your policies through our automated playbooks, but we’re capable of advising the highest levels in your organisation on operational risk management.

When you can’t outsource
